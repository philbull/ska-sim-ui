About SKA Simulations

Citing the simulations

If you make use of any of the simulations hosted here in a publication, please include the citations listed on the info pages of those simulations.

We would also appreciate an acknowledgement of using this hosting service, e.g. by including the following text in your acknowledgements: "We acknowledge use of the SKA Simulation Service, hosted by Queen Mary University of London".

Can I upload my simulation here?

If you have a simulation that is relevant to SKA and want to host it through this service, please get in touch with Phil Bull. We have a decent amount of space available to host SKA-related simulations, and can support several different database/file formats for the data.

Can I make my own mirror of the simulations?

Yes, as long as you get permission from the owners of the simulations you want to host. The web interface itself is open source, and can be downloaded from Git (TODO).
